Wilson's Promontory is a good four hour drive from Meloburne CBD. It is located at the south east part of Victoria.
Because of its unique flora and fauna, Wilson's Promontory is known as a popular tourist attraction.


If you are lucky, you might meet one of these cutie!


At Wilson's Promontory Wildlife Park, we have Tidal River Campground for you to relax with your family. We also have sand dunes and you can hire a van with our service provider and go Sand Surfing!


There will never be a dull day on this marvelous trip!
